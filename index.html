    What is Vastile?

    Vastile is a modified survival multiplayer server that aims to provide a one of a kind Minecraft multiplayer experience unlike any other. Our goal is to make Minecraft fun for all players even if they aren't interested in building which is an integral part of vanilla Minecraft. Venture into a vast blocky world full of secrets and surprises to discover alongside friends from around the world.
